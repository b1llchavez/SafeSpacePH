WHO WE ARE

SafeSpaces PH is a digital initiative committed to empowering victims of sexual harassment, abuse, and other violations under the Safe Spaces Act (RA 11313). We provide a platform that bridges the gap between marginalized individuals and pro bono legal professionals, ensuring that no one is denied the right to justice due to lack of access or resources.

We believe in a Philippines where everyone—regardless of gender, age, or background—can exist and move freely in public and private spaces without fear of harassment or violence.

OUR MISSION

To promote peace, justice, and strong institutions by ensuring that legal support is accessible, inclusive, and empowering, especially for the marginalized and vulnerable sectors of society.

OUR VISION

To build a Philippines where every individual—regardless of gender, background, or status—feels safe, heard, and protected, supported by strong institutions and accessible legal services that uphold justice, equality, and human dignity.

WHAT WE DO

Free Legal Appointments

Victims can book sessions with verified, volunteer legal professionals based on their situation.

Lawyer Collaboration

Licensed lawyers and legal groups can register to volunteer their services through our platform.

Reporting Made Accessible

Victims can submit violation reports and upload supporting documents securely.

Community-Driven Support

A team of legal professionals, developers, and advocates working together to promote safer institutions.
