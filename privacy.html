Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 12, 2025

SafeSpace PH is committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you access or use our platform

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data when you interact with the platform:

  • Personal details (e.g., name, age, email)
  • Case-related information you voluntarily provide
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, time on site)
  • Technical data (e.g., browser, IP address)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Connect users with appropriate volunteer lawyers
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Ensure security and detect misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Verified volunteer lawyers for case handling
  • Service providers assisting in operations (bound by confidentiality)
  • Legal authorities, when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Your Rights

Under RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), you have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent or request deletion (subject to legal exceptions)

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified via the platform or email of significant changes.
