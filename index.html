Welcome To SafeSpace PH

Connect with volunteer lawyers, know your rights, and access justice.

How to Report a Violation with SafeSpace PH

Step 1 Identify the Violation

Determine if the behavior you've experienced is a violation of the Safe Space Act.

Step 2 Document Evidence

Take screenshots, save messages or gather any proof that supports your report.

Step 3 Create an Account or Log In

Sign up for a new account or log in to your existing one.

Step 4 Fill Out the Report Form

Provide details about the incident and upload any evidence.

Step 5 Book an Appointment (Optional)

You may choose to book a free consultation with a volunteer lawyer.

Step 6 Wait for Confirmation

We'll notify you once your appointment is approved or if further action is needed.

Step 7 Coordinate with Your Lawyer

Communicate directly with your assigned lawyer for guidance and support.
